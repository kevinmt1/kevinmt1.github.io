THE STORY OF

~ danbam ~

Danbam restaurant has been around since the 20th century, started from a man who has visited various restaurants in order to taste the perfect taste of food. However, his satisfaction will feel the expected taste of the food that has not been fulfilled. His desire for original flavors, distinctive tastes, taste that he wanted to continue to taste, rich in spices, led him to start creating his own food. The man decided to open the Danbam restaurant out of his wish that other people too could taste a dish that could truly be said to be food.

Baek Jong-won

The Owner and Founder of Danbam Restaurant

Baek Jong-won is a Korean chef borned in 1967 at Seoul, South Korea. Borned from a Korean family, he learned a lot of traditional dishes from his family. He then studied culinary at Le Cordon Bleu, France where he focused on traditional Korean dishes. After graduated, he then go back to South Korea to open his own restaurant which he named Danbam. Danbam serves traditional Korean food which has authentic taste that is served in the modern way.
